Hey you!

Nice to meet you!
If you haven't already guessed, my name is Matilda! I'm 22 years old, born and raised in Skövde, Sweden. Ever since I was a little kid have I loved to paint and be creative as many children does, however my love affair with painting never stopped.

Little more than two years ago I attended a course in Illustration painting from Berghs Scool of Communication in Stockholm, and I've been hooked ever since! The last couple of years has been devoted to finding new ways and techniques to paint, and continuing on my creative path, hopefully I can inspire you to be creative to!

What I Do

I mainly use two techniques of painting, Illustrations and Acrylics. My illustration are made on an IPad Pro with Apple Pencil in the program Procreate. I love how free you can be, I can paint whereever I am! View my illustrations in the gallery!

My Illustrations

I also do acrylic paintings, on canvases in different sizes, it’s a great way to relax and just enjoy the moment. I love to paint acrylic paintings together with friends and family, it’s a great way to spend time together and be creative. Get inspired by my acrylic paintings in the gallery!

Custom paintings

The easiest way to order a painting is to either use the contact page, or dm me on Instagram. If you'd like a custom illustration I'll need a photo to paint by, once I've got that, I can get back to you with a quote. Prices varies and are based on the level of details and scope of the painting. Get in touch and we'll sure figure something out that suits your need and occasion.
