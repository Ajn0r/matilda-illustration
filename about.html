"Five years ago, I enrolled in an advanced painting class at the San Francisco Art Institute through its Adult Continuing Education Program. But a decade later, in an art studio whose ghosts included Richard Diebenkorn and Elmer Bischoff, I picked up a paintbrush, and began painting."
